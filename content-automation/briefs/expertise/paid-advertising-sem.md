## 4. Representative Projects & Use Cases

**Example 1: SaaS (Search & Retargeting)**

*   **Context:** A SaaS company was struggling to generate qualified leads from its search campaigns.
*   **Business Problem:** Low click-through rates and high cost-per-acquisition.
*   **Strategy:** Developed a highly targeted search campaign, focusing on long-tail keywords and utilizing dynamic keyword insertion. Implemented retargeting campaigns to re-engage website visitors who hadn't converted.
*   **Execution:** Managed Google Ads campaigns, created ad copy, set up bidding strategies, and implemented retargeting pixels.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased lead conversion rates by 30% and reduced cost-per-lead by 20%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Precise targeting and retargeting are crucial for maximizing ROI in search campaigns.

**Example 2: Fintech (Social Media Lead Generation)**

*   **Context:** A Fintech company needed to generate leads for a new loan product.
*   **Business Problem:** Limited brand awareness and low engagement on social media.
*   **Strategy:** Launched a targeted social media campaign, promoting the loan product to high-income earners. Utilized lead generation forms and paid social advertising to capture leads.
*   **Execution:** Created engaging social media content, ran paid campaigns on LinkedIn and Facebook, and implemented lead generation forms.
*   **Outcomes:** Generated over 500 qualified leads and increased brand awareness by 15%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Social media can be a powerful tool for lead generation when targeted effectively.

**Example 3: E-commerce (Display Retargeting)**

*   **Context:** An e-commerce company wanted to recover abandoned shopping carts.
*   **Business Problem:** High cart abandonment rates.
*   **Strategy:** Implemented a display retargeting campaign, showing personalized product recommendations to users who had previously viewed items in their cart.
*   **Execution:** Set up Google Display Network campaigns, created visual ads, and targeted users based on their browsing behavior.
*   **Outcomes:** Recovered 10% of abandoned shopping carts and increased sales by 5%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Retargeting is an effective way to recover lost sales and drive repeat purchases.
