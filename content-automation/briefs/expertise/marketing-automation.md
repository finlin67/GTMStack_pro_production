## 4. Representative Projects & Use Cases

**Example 1: SaaS (Lead Nurturing)**

*   **Context:** A SaaS company was struggling to convert marketing leads into sales opportunities.
*   **Business Problem:** Leads were falling through the cracks due to manual follow-up and inconsistent messaging.
*   **Strategy:** Developed a multi-stage lead nurturing workflow, triggered by website activity and email engagement, incorporating personalized email sequences and targeted content.
*   **Execution:** Configured HubSpot, built email templates, and set up automated workflows to deliver the right content to the right leads at the right time.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased lead conversion rates by 25% and reduced sales cycle length by 10%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** A well-designed lead nurturing workflow, triggered by behavioral data, can dramatically improve lead conversion rates.

**Example 2: Fintech (Customer Onboarding)**

*   **Context:** A Fintech company was experiencing high customer churn rates after onboarding.
*   **Business Problem:** Customers were struggling to understand the product and needed ongoing support.
*   **Strategy:** Created an automated onboarding workflow, delivering personalized tutorials, FAQs, and support resources based on customer usage and questions.
*   **Execution:** Integrated Marketo with Salesforce, built email sequences, and created interactive onboarding guides.
*   **Outcomes:** Reduced customer churn by 15% and improved customer satisfaction scores.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Automated onboarding workflows can significantly improve customer engagement and reduce churn.

**Example 3: E-commerce (Post-Purchase Email)**

*   **Context:** An e-commerce company wanted to increase repeat purchases from existing customers.
*   **Business Problem:** They lacked a system for automatically sending personalized post-purchase emails.
*   **Strategy:** Designed a workflow that triggered emails based on purchase history, offering product recommendations, discounts, and loyalty rewards.
*   **Execution:** Implemented Klaviyo, created email templates, and set up automated triggers.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased repeat purchase rates by 18% and boosted average order value by 8%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Automated post-purchase email campaigns can be a highly effective way to drive repeat business.
