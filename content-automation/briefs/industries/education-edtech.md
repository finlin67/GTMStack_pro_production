**Representative Projects & Use Cases**

**Example 1: Higher Education – Recruitment Campaign**

*   **Context:** A university seeking to increase enrollment in a new graduate program.
*   **Business Problem:** Low awareness of the program and limited applicant interest.
*   **Strategy:** Developed a targeted recruitment campaign leveraging digital channels, including social media, online advertising, and email marketing.
*   **Execution:** Created a compelling video showcasing the program’s benefits, launched a social media campaign targeting prospective students, and implemented an online application process.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased enrollment in the program by 20%.
*   **Transferable Insight:** Targeted digital marketing campaigns are effective for driving enrollment in higher education programs.

**Example 2: K-12 – School Branding**

*   **Context:** A K-12 school district seeking to enhance its brand image and attract new families.
*   **Business Problem:** Low brand recognition and limited enrollment.
*   **Strategy:** Developed a comprehensive branding strategy, defining the school district’s mission, values, and key differentiators.
*   **Execution:** Created a new logo, developed a website, and implemented a social media strategy.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased brand awareness and expanded the school district’s reach.
*   **Transferable Insight:** A strong brand identity is essential for attracting families and building a positive school culture.

**Example 3: EdTech – Product Launch**

*   **Context:** An EdTech company launching a new online learning platform.
*   **Business Problem:** Low adoption rates and limited user engagement.
*   **Strategy:** Developed a go-to-market strategy focused on targeted marketing, content creation, and user onboarding.
*   **Execution:** Created a series of online tutorials, launched a social media campaign, and implemented a user onboarding process.
*   **Outcomes:** Increased adoption rates and user engagement.
*   **Transferable Insight:** A well-executed go-to-market strategy is essential for the success of new EdTech products.
