Frequently Asked Questions

How long before we see pipeline impact? ▼
Technical optimizations typically yield results in 4-6 weeks, while new content clusters generally hit peak performance within 3-5 months depending on domain authority.
Do you handle the actual content writing? ▼
Yes, our team includes B2B SaaS specialist writers. We interview your subject matter experts to ensure the content is high-quality and authoritative.
How do you track CRM attribution? ▼
We use multi-touch attribution models and set up specific UTM and hidden field capturing to ensure organic sessions are logged against Lead and Contact records.
Do you work with headless CMS setups? ▼
Absolutely. We are highly experienced in technical SEO for Next.js, Gatsby, and modern headless CMS environments like Contentful and Sanity.
What is your primary SEO metric? ▼
While we track rankings and traffic, our primary North Star metric is Organic Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) and Pipeline Generated.
