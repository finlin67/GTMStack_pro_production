# How to Use GTMStack.pro

## 1) What This Website Is

GTMStack.pro is a hybrid **portfolio + consulting agency website** for a founder-led B2B Go-To-Market (GTM) practice.

It is designed to do two things at once:
- Show proof of expertise and real outcomes (portfolio function)
- Help qualified visitors understand, trust, and engage consulting services (agency function)

In short: this is a site for decision-makers who want to build a more predictable, scalable revenue engine.

---

## 2) Who This Website Is For

### Primary audience
- Founders and CEOs of B2B tech companies
- CMOs, VPs of Marketing, and Growth leaders
- Revenue Operations and Demand Generation leaders
- Teams preparing to scale from early traction to repeatable pipeline

### Company types that get the most value
- B2B SaaS and enterprise software companies
- FinTech, HealthTech, AI/ML, developer tools, and cybersecurity businesses
- Organizations with strong products but inconsistent GTM execution

### Typical pain points this audience has
- Pipeline volume is unstable despite active marketing spend
- Strategy exists but execution is fragmented
- Martech and data are disconnected across systems
- Marketing cannot confidently show revenue attribution
- Teams need both strategic direction and hands-on implementation

## 8) Quick Evaluation Checklist for New Visitors

Use this checklist while browsing, organized by what you're evaluating:

### Industry Fit
- [ ] Your company's industry is represented on the site (or close enough to transfer learning)
- [ ] The industry page accurately describes your market realities and buying behavior
- [ ] The featured expertise and playbooks are relevant to your GTM challenges

### Capability Fit
- [ ] The expertise pages cover the specific functional areas you need support in
- [ ] The "modern plays" described reflect your GTM aspirations (not your current constraints)
- [ ] The depth of knowledge across 15+ specialties suggests true GTM systems thinking

### Outcome Alignment
- [ ] At least 2-3 case studies show results similar to your goals (pipeline, conversion, efficiency)
- [ ] The client types in case studies are comparable to your business model (SaaS, enterprise, etc.)
- [ ] You believe the outcomes are achievable in your context within 6-12 months

### Philosophy/Approach Fit
- [ ] The approach is **systems thinking** (holistic) rather than campaign-focused
- [ ] The methodology emphasizes **measurement and attribution**, not vanity metrics
- [ ] The work is positioned as **strategic + hands-on implementation**, not just advisory

### Engagement Readiness
- [ ] You're confident your leadership would agree to discuss transformation
- [ ] You have budget flexibility or can make business case for GTM investment
- [ ] You're ready to commit 4-12 weeks to discovery and planning before engagement starts

**Scoring:**
- **9-10 checks:** Strong fit. You're ready to start a discovery conversation.
- **7-8 checks:** Good fit. Review a few more case studies or industry details before contacting.
- **5-6 checks:** Potential fit. Clarify which expertise areas and industries align best.
- **<5 checks:** Not yet a fit. Consider revisiting in 6-12 months as priorities evolve.

## 9) Content Architecture Quick Reference

The site is designed so **expertise, industries, and case studies interconnect**. Here's how to navigate these connections:

### From an Expertise Page
- **See related case studies** — Each expertise area links to 2-4 relevant client examples
- **See industry context** — Links to industry pages where that expertise applies
- **Compare** — Browse related expertise areas to see how they work together

### From an Industry Page  
- **See featured expertise** — Links to the 5-7 specialties most relevant to that vertical
- **See featured case studies** — Links to 2-4 examples from that industry or business model
- **Understand the playbook** — Short overview of proven GTM approaches in that market

### From a Case Study
- **See featured expertise** — Links to all specialties involved in the engagement
- **See industry context** — Link to the vertical/industry the client operates in
- **Compare** — Browse other case studies using similar expertise

### Navigation Examples

**Scenario 1: "We need ABM but let's check if our vertical matters"**
→ Account-Based Marketing (expertise) → Click related industries → Review featured case studies in your vertical

**Scenario 2: "We're a B2B SaaS company. What can GTM transformation look like?"**
→ B2B SaaS (industry) → Review playbook → Explore featured expertise areas → Read all featured case studies

**Scenario 3: "We're struggling with data/analytics. How deep does this go?"**
→ Marketing Analytics & Reporting (expertise) → Or Revenue Operations → Read BI & Data Engineering → See Salesforce case study
