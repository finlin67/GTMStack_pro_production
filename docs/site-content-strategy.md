# Site Content Strategy Reference

Project: GTMStack.pro Website Content Strategy

Version: 1.0

Date: October 26, 2023

1. Overall Goal: To drive qualified leads and conversions by clearly showcasing GTMStack.pro’s expertise in revenue operations and strategic go-to-market consulting.

2. Site Hierarchy & Landing Page Structure:

Homepage (Landing Page 1 - “The Hub”) – Primary CTA: View Strategy
Purpose: Introduces GTMStack.pro, highlights key services, and directs users to relevant sections.
Content: Hero section (as designed), Overview of Expertise (grid), Featured Case Studies (carousel), Strategy Planner preview.
Expertise (Mega Menu - Landing Page 2 - “Our Frameworks”) – Primary CTA: Explore Experience
Purpose: Provides a deep dive into the four core service pillars.
Content: 4-column mega menu with detailed descriptions and visuals for each pillar (Content & Engagement, Demand & Growth, Strategy & Insights, Systems & Operations).
Industries (Landing Page 3 - “Industry Focus”) – Secondary CTA: Explore Case Studies
Purpose: Highlights industries served, demonstrating relevant experience.
Content: Grid of industries with brief descriptions and links to relevant case studies.
Projects (Landing Page 4 - “Proven Results”) – Secondary CTA: Request a Consultation
Purpose: Showcases successful projects and quantifiable results.
Content: Carousel of case studies with detailed results.
Strategy Planner (Landing Page 5 - “The Tool”) – Primary CTA: Request a Demo
Purpose: Introduces the proprietary Strategy Planner application.
Content: Full-width screenshot, description of functionality, and a call to action to request a demo.
Contact (Landing Page 6 - “Get in Touch”) – Secondary CTA: Schedule a Call
Purpose: Provides contact information and encourages direct engagement.
Content: Contact form, email address, phone number, and links to social media profiles.
3. Visual Grouping within the UI:

Expertise Section: The 4-column mega menu clearly groups services by function, creating a logical and easily navigable structure. The color palette (Teal, Cyan, Grey) visually reinforces the categorization.
Case Studies: Presented in a grid and carousel format, allowing for easy browsing and filtering based on industry or service.
Hero Section: Strategic placement of the “View Strategy” CTA above the Expertise section, guiding the user to the core value proposition.
4. Primary Calls-to-Action (CTAs):

“View Strategy” (Homepage): The primary CTA – drives users to the most important piece of content.
“Explore Experience” (Expertise Mega Menu): Encourages deeper engagement with the service pillars.
“Request a Demo” (Strategy Planner): Targets users interested in the tool and its capabilities.
“Request a Consultation” (Projects): Encourages users to explore the benefits of GTMStack.pro’s services.
5. Intended User Journey (Structural Perspective):

Initial Impression: User lands on the Homepage and is immediately presented with the brand’s core offering – strategic go-to-market consulting.
Exploration of Expertise: The user navigates to the “Expertise” section to understand the specific services offered.
Validation & Proof: The user explores the “Industries” and “Projects” sections to see relevant case studies and demonstrate the value proposition.
Deep Dive & Tool Introduction: The user explores the “Strategy Planner” to understand the tool’s capabilities and request a demo.
Contact & Engagement: The user contacts GTMStack.pro through the “Contact” page.
6. Measurement & Iteration:

Key metrics to track: Bounce rate, time on site, CTA click-through rates, lead generation.
Regularly review and adjust the content strategy based on these metrics.
